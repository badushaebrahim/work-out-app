KINETIC

Incline
Dumbbell Press Target: Upper Pectorals
Sets 04
Reps 12
Rest 60s

Detailed Instructions

01

Set the Foundation

Adjust the bench to a 30-45 degree incline. Sit with your back firmly against the pad and feet planted wide for maximum stability.

02

The Drive Phase

Press the weights upward in a controlled arc, converging them slightly at the top without touching. Keep your shoulders retracted.

03

Controlled Eccentric

Lower the dumbbells slowly until they are level with your chest, feeling a deep stretch in the upper pectorals. Maintain tension throughout.

format_quote Trainer's Elite Insight

"Focus on the stretch at the bottom of the movement. That's where the muscle fiber recruitment happens for that upper-chest shelf. Don't ego lift—control is king."
